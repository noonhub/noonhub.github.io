The 2019 Ski Cabin Olympics Events & Schedule

10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony @ Le Chamois


  • Mandatory attendance for all participants to hear rules
  • Das boot / team pitcher chug. Teams of 4

All Day - Flag Football @ Everywhere


  • All competitors wear flag football belt during events.
  • Any competitor can steal a flag from an opponent at any point during events.
  • Can only steal one flag from a single person per event.
  • Flags cannot be stolen while in lift line or on a lift.
  • Team flag count at end of day determines places.
  • Final flag count will be done just before Gelande Quaffing.

10:30 AM - Dizzy Pole KT Lap @ KT 22 Chair


  • Teams of 2 - one competitor starts up top, one starts below.
  • Load KT, at top meet teammate to spin around ski pole 10 times and chug a beer/canned wine/ other canned beverage.
  • Ski to base and cross the finish line.

11:15 PM - Outfit Swap Lap @ Squaw One (if open) or KT 22 Chair


  • Teams of 2
  • Load Squaw One or KT with teammate.
  • Cross the finish line having swapped outfits from top to bottom - includes all visible items of clothing except for boots & skis
  • Judges encouraged to take pictures of teams before and after in order to judge proper outfit swapping.

12:00 PM - Search and Rescue Beer @ KT 22 Chair


  • Teams of 2
  • Load KT w/ teammate and go to defined area ( within 20 meters of a specific lift tower).
  • Perform search and rescue with beacon to find beer.
  • Chug beer and ski to the finish line.

12:45 PM - Team Flip Cup @ Chammy


  • Teams of 4
  • GTFO if you dont know how to play flip cup!

1:30 PM - Gelande Quaffing @ Chammy


  • 4 person teams, no subs.
  • 1 minute for first 3 rounds, finals are 2 minutes.
  • Quaffer must be 2 feet from the edge of the bar.
  • Must rotate pitcher and quaffer every pitch.
  • You CANNOT pitch until beer is down and mug touches the bar again!
  • You must quaff the beer, not wear it.
  • No puking, puking results in immediate team disqualification.
  • In the event of a tie, each team will choose 1 quaffer and 1 pitcher, and the team with the fastest Gelande Quaffing
    Scoring
  • Round 1: 1 point regular catch, 2 points handle catch.
  • Round 2: The quaffer must do a 360 between leaving the pitchers hand and the quaffer’s catch. 1 point regular catch, 2 points handle catch.
  • Round 3: The quaffer must to a 360 OR under the leg catch. 1 point regular catch, 2 points handle catch.
  • Finals: (2min) 1 catch of each… handle catch, 360 catch, and under the leg catch. After the 3 required catches…Freestyle. 0 points regular catch*, 1 point handle catch, 2 points 360 handle catch* OR under the leg, 0-5 points freestyle (at judges discretion)

2:15 PM - Closing Ceremony & Dance Party @ Chammy


  • Winners shall be crowned and losers shammed
